Who is Shella Mae?

Welcome to my autobiography website!I am delighted to have you here, joining me on this virtual exploration of my life's story.This website is a testament to my unwavering passion for sharing my experiences and inspiring others through the lessons I have learned along my unique journey.

A girl who always live her life to the fullest and making the best out of everything. I am Shella Mae Caballar Tabuna, a 20 year old who was born on a sunny day of October 5th year 2003. I am the youngest and gorgeous daughter of Sergio Tabuna and Liza Tabuna. I have one sister and two brothers. Currently residing at Tabugon, one of the barangays in Santa Fe.

As you navigate through the pages of my autobiography website, you will uncover a rich compilation of captivating stories, cherished hobbies, and profound life lessons. I will share the moments of triumph and joy, illuminate my educational endeavors, and unveil the transformative growth that has shaped my identity. It is my sincere hope that through this digital odyssey, I can ignite inspiration, provide entertainment, and forge meaningful connections with each and every one of you.

So, fasten your seatbelts and embark on this exhilarating journey with me. Together, let us revel in the beauty of life and celebrate the power of shared experiences. Enjoy the voyage!

Personal Life

2015-2016

I started my education journey at Tabugon Elementary School. Becoming a salutatorian was a crowning achievement in my elementary days. It was a transformative chapter in my life and instilled in me a love for learning, a drive for excellence, and the belief that with hard work and dedication, nothing is impossible.

2020-2021

I spent my high school days at Tranquilino Cawaling Sr. National High School and finished my senior high school at Santa Fe National High School-Senior High School. I pursued the HUMSS track and graduated with honors. It nurtured my intellectual curiosity, fostered creativity, and instilled a deep appreciation in me.

2022-Present

Today, I am a 2nd-year student at Romblon State University, taking a Bachelor of Science in Information Technology. As a BSIT student, I begin to navigate the ever-evolving digital landscape. It has nurtured my passion for technology, honed my problem-solving abilities, and equipped me with the skills needed to thrive in the digital era.

Wandering

As I grew older, nature became my sanctuary. I always find solace in the embrace of forests, the healing rhythm of waves, and the serenity of starlit nights. In the presence of nature, stress melts away, problems seem smaller, and a sense of calm and tranquility washes over me. It always reminds me of the inherent harmony and resilience of the natural world.

Dancing

Dancing is not merely a hobby, but a profound interest that breathes life into my existence. Since I was young, I already have a passion for dancing. I always joined dancing competitions and was able to represent my school. Through dance, I find solace, freedom, and a sense of purpose. Dancing is not just an interest; it is the very essence of my being..

Photography

I love taking pictures of nature, spellbound by its enchanting beauty, especially when the sun dips below the horizon, casting its warm glow across the landscape. Each click of the shutter is a testament to my deep connection with the natural world, as I strive to encapsulate the fleeting beauty and serene tranquility of sunsets.

Childhood Friend

Childhood friends are the benchmark of our lives. Elizabeth Cawaling is one of my childhood friends with whom I am still together. She has seen me at my worst and best, but never judges me. We have been friends since elementary until now. She is one of the ones I met early on and share the most memories with. The one I'll always remember.

University Friend

In school, we learned lessons from teachers, but our true education came from the friendships we made. College is stressful but God gave them to me which makes the journey worthwhile. They became the pillars of support and the cheerleaders of our dreams. Also, they make my college days memorable and extraordinary.

Group Friend

Group friends are like puzzle pieces. Each one brings something unique, but together, they create a beautiful picture. This circle of friends is my companion when it comes to nature escapades, drinking, and when it comes in adventures. Exploring nature with friends is like discovering a hidden treasure together, creating memories that will last a lifetime.

Achievements and Awards

I became an academic achiever when I was elementary until mid-school. Graduating with honors, I have showcased my exceptional academic abilities and dedication to learning. With an insatiable curiosity and a drive for success, I have not only gotten outstanding grades but also actively participated in academic competitions and earned recognition, such as being a champion in the MTAP competition. I'm also active in extra-curricular activities, and one of the awards I garnered was being GSP of the Year. Through this, I want to inspire my fellow students and be a shining example of what can be achieved through dedication and a love for learning.
Growing up, I have always been captivated by the world of beauty and its ability to empower individuals. I started joining pageant when I was 13 years old. I still remember the feeling of competing for the first time. It was a great experience that became a driving force for me to join the world of pageantry. I won the title of Miss TCSNHS 2019, Miss Chapel 2022, and my recently, Miss SK Tabugon 2023. As I embark on this incredible journey towards the pageant stage, I am filled with excitement and gratitude. I am eager to use this platform to amplify my voice, inspire others, and make a positive impact.
From a young age, I discovered the magic of dance. It ignited a fire within me, awakening my love for movement and rhythm. In my first year, I joined the folk dance audition in our institute and was gladly selected. Through it, I was able to be a part of the folk dance team that represents our university at the STRASUC Festival held at Batangas State University in 2022. Additionally, I also became a part again of the folk dance team that represents the university at Occidental Mindoro State College in 2023. In those two competitions, we got the 3rd place. The experiences, friendships, and knowledge gained during practice have shaped me into the person I am today.
This award is an award for myself as life is a rollercoaster, and I have faced my fair share of challenges along the way. Anxiety and sadness have been constant companions throughout my life. The weight of academic pressures, expectations, and the fear of failure often felt overwhelming. These emotions created a cloud of self-doubt and hindered my ability to fully embrace life's opportunities. However, I refused to let these shadows define me. It hasn't been an easy road, but I am proud of the progress I have made. Remember, you are not alone, and with determination, support, and self-compassion, you can rise above the shadows and achieve your goals.
